### Section 1: "Universal capture"

*Theme: Universal Capture.*

**Label:** Universal capture
**Value prop:** Capture every meeting, everywhere.

**Features:**

- **Desktop-based local capture** — Records using your system audio. No intrusive bot in the meeting. Works across every major platform.

- **Automatic recording** — Grain detects when you join a call and starts capturing. No manual setup.

- **Bot recording (optional)** — For teams that want video recording with in-meeting presence, the Grain bot joins your calendar meetings automatically.

- **100+ languages** — AI-powered transcription with precise speaker identification in over 100 languages.

---

### Section 2: "Context-enriched transcripts"

*Theme: Meeting Context Enrichment.*

**Label:** Context-enriched transcripts
**Value prop:** Grain enriches your transcripts with speaker identity, relationship history, and cross-meeting context — so your AI has the full picture, not just raw text.

**Features:**

- **Meeting templates** — Tailor your notes for every meeting type with custom or pre-built templates.

- **Ask AI across meetings** — Ask questions across your entire meeting library and generate reports with precise citations.

- **Clips & highlights** — Select text to create shareable video clips. Organize collections with playlists and stories.

- **Trackers** — Monitor key terms, competitive mentions, and important phrases across all your meetings.

---

### Section 3: "AI agent integration"

*Theme: Universal AI Access.*

**Label:** AI agent integration
**Value prop:** Load your meeting data into Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI tool — so your agents can do the work that comes after the meeting.

**Features:**

- **Grain MCP Server** — Connect your meeting data directly to Claude, Cursor, and other AI tools. Query transcripts, search across meetings, and pull context into any MCP-compatible agent.

- **Open in Claude / ChatGPT** — One-click deep links that open your enriched transcript directly in your AI tool of choice. No downloading, no pasting.

- **Enriched Markdown download** — Full transcripts with speaker IDs, timestamps, and context metadata in clean Markdown — ready for any AI workflow.

- **Grain API** — Build automated workflows and custom integrations. Push meeting data into any system programmatically.

- **Bulk context export** — Copy transcripts across multiple meetings, clips, or playlists at once. Give your AI the full picture across an entire project or relationship.

## Tone & Voice Notes

- **Lead with outcomes, not features.** "Feed your AI the full picture" > "Download Markdown transcripts."
- **Assume AI fluency.** The ICP knows what Claude, ChatGPT, and MCP are. Don't over-explain.
- **Avoid "notetaker" as primary identity.** Use it as table-stakes context, not the headline.
- **Avoid sales-team-specific language.** No "deal records," "coaching scorecards," or "pipeline" unless in a secondary use case page.
- **Be direct and confident.** This ICP values tools that respect their intelligence. No fluff.
